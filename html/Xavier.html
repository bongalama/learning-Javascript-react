A Rock Choir Membership Gives You…

⭐ The UK’s original and award-winning, local, contemporary choir experience.

⭐ An exciting and life-affirming journey building new friendships and experiencing unique events.

⭐ An opportunity to engage with your community and support local and national charities.

⭐ A fresh understanding of music and development of new skills with specially created audio and visual resources.

⭐ An increase in personal confidence and improvement in your general well-being.

⭐ Performances, experiences and social events created for your chosen choir.

⭐ Unlimited weekly rehearsals in over 400 other available locations across The United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.

⭐ Professional & qualified musicians, singers and teachers trained by Rock Choir who deliver all tuition and experiences.

⭐ Invitations to attend National Rock Choir events e.g. Wembley Arena, the NEC, London O2 & Liverpool Echo Arena.

⭐ Access to a wide variety of workshops and experiences such as Abbey Road Studios recording experiences, performance tours abroad (Disneyland Paris / Switzerland / Bruges) and more!

⭐ Unique vocal versions of classic and current pop & rock songs created only for Rock Choir by the Rock Choir Team.

Plus access to our ‘Rock Choir Extras’ such as:

⭐ Free entry to Vocal Yoga, designed to promote wellness and improve vocal technique.

⭐ Free entry to ‘Rock & Sign’, specially designed to offer Rock Choir members the chance to learn sign language using the Rock Choir repertoire.

⭐ A Free 3 month trial period with ‘Rest Less (formerly Mirthy)’, the UK’s fastest growing digital community for the over 50’s.
